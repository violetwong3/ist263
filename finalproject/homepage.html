Doing art is like reconnecting with an old friend—not only the finished product but the ambiance of the smell of pencils and the sound of sharpening them with a knife and the choosing and blending of colors and materials. The process of turning lines and colors into something beautiful felt magical.
When I see something striking -- a person, a face, a flower, a nature scene -- I just want to capture it.
